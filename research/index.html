Research

September 1, 2020 . Chih-Kai Chang

I am a first year Ph.D. student at Arizona State University in Industrial Engineering Department, working as a research assistant in Manufacturing and Service Automation Lab. I received my Bachelor degree in Industrial Engineering and Industrial Management with minor in Economics at National Tsing Hua University in Hsinchu-Taiwan, and my Master degree in Biostatistics at Johns Hopkins University in Baltimore-USA.

As for my work experience, I worked as a bussiness data analyst at GfK-Taipei for a year, and a data scientist contractor at Tomofun-Taipei before joining ASU.

In my free time, I watch sports and play video game. I am also a big fan of baseball.
